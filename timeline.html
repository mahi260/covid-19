How did the coronavirus spread?

  • December 8A patient in the city of Wuhan sought medical help for pneumonia-like symptoms.
  • December 29Local hospitals in Hubei report the first four cases of a "pneumonia of unknown etiology".
  • December 31Wuhan municipal health authorities report a string of pneumonia-like cases in Wuhan,China.
  • Januaru 1The seafood market(Huanan seafood wholesale market) that was identified as a suspected center of the outbreak is closed.
  • January 3China officially notifies the WHO of an outbreak.
  • January 9WHO announces mysterious coronavirus-related Pneumonia in Wuhan,China.
  • January 13Thailand reports its first case, a sixty-one year old woman who did not visit hte seafood market.
  • January 14Wuhan implements screening measures for travelers leaving the city at airports,railway stations and other passenger terminals.
  • January 21CDC confirms first US coronavirus case and chinese scientist confirms covid-19 human transmission.
  • January 23Wuhan now under quarantine.
  • January 31WHO issues global health emergency due to the exponential jump of more than 9800 cases and more than 200 deaths had been confirmed worldwide.
  • February 3First cases appear in many more countries, travel bans spread.
